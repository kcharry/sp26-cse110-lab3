    (called "pomodoros"), separated by 5-minute breaks
  • "Pomodoro" is the Italian word for tomato. Literally translated, it means "golden apple" (composed of pomo,
    meaning "apple/fruit," and d’oro, meaning "of gold").
  • Site will be broken up into three pages, main pomodoro 25 min timer, the short 5 min break timer, and then long 15 min
